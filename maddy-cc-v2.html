Morning Open Checklist

  • Unlock front door, turn on lights & computers
  • Check voicemail and return any missed calls
  • Pull up schedule and review today's appointments
  • Print or load intake forms for new patients
  • Confirm all Day 1 appointments and send reminders
  • Verify insurance for scheduled patients
  • Restock front desk supplies if needed

Patient Intake Workflow

  • Greet patient warmly — use their first name
  • Hand off intake paperwork or iPad for digital forms
  • Scan / photograph completed intake form
  • Enter patient demographics into EHR
  • Collect insurance card front & back
  • Verify coverage before patient is roomed
  • Notify Dr. Stutz when patient is ready
  • Upload intake form to D1 Recon (see below)

Phone Scripts

  • "Thank you for calling Lone Star Chiropractic — how can I help you today?"
  • For new patients: collect name, DOB, chief complaint, insurance, preferred time
  • For scheduling: confirm date, time, provider, and send a text reminder
  • For billing questions: log the issue, pull the account, escalate to Dr. Stutz if needed

End of Day Wrap-Up

  • Post all charges from today's visits
  • Reconcile end-of-day payments and receipts
  • Confirm tomorrow's schedule is complete
  • Send any outstanding intake reminders
  • Lock up and arm the alarm

D1 Recon — Patient Prep Upload

Your job: Upload the completed patient intake form to D1 Recon before or immediately after the patient is roomed. This lets Dr. Stutz review it before the consultation.
  1. Scan or photograph the completed intake form (PDF preferred, JPG/PNG also accepted)
  2. Go to the D1 Recon page using the button below
  3. Enter the patient's name in the "Patient Name" field
  4. Click "Intake Form (Maddy)" and select the file
  5. Wait for the green checkmark — upload complete
  6. Let Dr. Stutz know it's uploaded if he's with another patient
Tip: Name the file with the patient's last name and date — e.g., Smith_2026-06-30_intake.pdf — so it's easy to find later.
