Reactivation Outreach — Daily

  • Pull list of patients inactive 30–90 days from EHR
  • Prioritize patients with open care plans or missed milestones
  • Call 10–15 patients per day — log all attempts
  • Send follow-up text if no answer after 2 calls
  • Offer 3 scheduling options when patient answers
  • Log outcome in EHR: scheduled, no answer, not interested, etc.
  • Update Dr. Stutz on conversion rate weekly

Reactivation Call Scripts

Opening
"Hi [Name], this is Sabrina calling from Lone Star Chiropractic. I was just thinking about you and wanted to check in — how have you been feeling since your last visit?"
Re-engage
"We have some time this week if you'd like to come back in. Dr. Stutz always asks about patients he hasn't seen in a while — you were making great progress."
Close
"Can I go ahead and get you on the schedule? I have Tuesday at 10 or Thursday at 2 available."

Office Management — Weekly

  • Reconcile accounts receivable and flag any 60+ day balances
  • Review next week's schedule for gaps — fill with reactivation calls
  • Submit insurance claims for previous week's visits
  • Follow up on outstanding claim denials or rejections
  • Check supply inventory — order if below par
  • Update patient satisfaction tracking sheet
  • Review and respond to any Google or Facebook reviews

Key Metrics to Track

  • Reactivation call attempts per day (goal: 10–15)
  • Reactivation conversion rate (goal: 20–30%)
  • Patient visit average (PVA) for the week
  • Collection rate: collected vs. billed
  • New patient referral sources — ask every new patient
  • Cancellation & no-show rate (report to Dr. Stutz)

D1 Recon — SHS Upload

Your job: Upload the Subjective Health Survey (SHS) for Day 1 patients. This is completed before or during the consultation and gives Dr. Stutz key intake data before he reviews the full file.
What is the SHS? The Subjective Health Survey is the symptom/history questionnaire completed by the patient. It should be scanned or exported as PDF and uploaded here as soon as it's complete.
  1. Collect or receive the completed SHS form from the patient (paper or digital)
  2. Scan or save as PDF
  3. Go to D1 Recon using the button below
  4. Enter the patient's name, then find the "SHS (Sabrina)" upload slot
  5. Upload the file and confirm the green checkmark appears
